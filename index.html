Projects

My contributions towards projects.

As a dedicated Engineering enthusiast, I've played a pivotal role in driving transformative projects forward, contributing with precision and problem-solving.

EcoRudder

The Fraunhofer Institute's research endeavors focus on the development of more sustainable vertical tailplanes for short- and medium-range aircraft. This initiative aims to innovate lightweight, eco-friendly materials, and manufacturing processes for these critical components, with the overarching objective of reducing the environmental footprint of air travel. By pioneering greener alternatives, this research contributes to advancing sustainability in the aviation industry.

HyDDEn

The TUM Institute for Advanced Study is leading the HYDDEN Project (Hydrogen Development and Deployment ENhancement), which focuses on advancing hydrogen storage systems. This research aims to develop innovative solutions to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of hydrogen storage, crucial for the widespread adoption of hydrogen as a clean energy source. Through collaborative efforts, the project strives to drive forward the transition towards a more sustainable and renewable energy future.
